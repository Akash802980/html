My Favorite Hobby: Cricket

Why I Like It

Cricket is more than just a game to me; it is a passion that keeps me energized. I enjoy this hobby because it teaches the importance of teamwork, patience, and strategic thinking.

How I Spend Time Doing It

I usually dedicate my weekends to playing matches with my friends at the local ground. My routine involves waking up early for a warm-up session followed by a few competitive net practices.
